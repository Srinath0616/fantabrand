Flavour Updated

Fanta has recently introduced three new zero-sugar flavors: Apple, Raspberry, and a limited-edition Tutti Frutti. The Tutti Frutti flavor combines banana, peach, and pineapple. These new additions are part of Fanta's commitment to offering a wider variety of fruity flavors with zero sugar, zero calories. The Tutti Frutti flavor will be available for a limited time in a sleek, colorful can.
